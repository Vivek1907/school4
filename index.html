About us

Sree Cauvery School

Sree Cauvery School at Indiranagar stands both as a symbol of integrity and also as a tribute to the Kodavas as an enlightened community with an immense love and respect for education.

Let us retrace our path. The pioneering steps taken by great luminaries like the former Chief Minister of Karnataka, Late Sri. R. Gundu Rao , Late Lt. Col. Koothanda M. Chengappa and many hands seen and unseen toiled to build this institution to what it stands today.

From a humble beginning with forty students in 1982, today 37 years later the mammoth structure replete with laboratories and an auditorium houses 1439 students.

The school is managed under the guidance of the Kodava Samaja Education Council.

Sree Cauvery School holds its pride in flaunting its excellence in the field of academics, sports, co-curricular activities and state of art technology in classrooms.

The level of academic success achieved by our students in all classes is good, particularly so at the Board level examinations ie, always 100% results or results tending towards it. Today we have established ourselves as a Bench Mark Institution of Learning.
